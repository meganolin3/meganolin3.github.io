Apr 2025

Rethinking Plant-Based Dining At UBC

A data-driven marketing strategy developed to increase adoption of plant-based meals in residence dining halls. Using primary survey research, consumer segmentation, competitive benchmarking, and behavioral science frameworks, the project identified key barriers including taste perceptions, low visibility, and convenience over sustainability motivation. These insights informed measurable objectives and a set of interventions focused on menu design, default options, experiential sampling, and social engagement event. The report delivers an actionable, KPI-driven roadmap designed to shift student behavior through positive reinforcement rather than environmental guilt.

Airbnb In Vancouver

A Tableau data visualization project focused on exploring listing patterns, pricing dynamics, and review behavior across Vancouver neighbourhoods. Using interactive dashboards, the analysis applies dual-axis charts, filters, reference lines, and calculated fields to examine average prices, listing growth over time, price distributions, review activity, and host concentration. The project then translates exploratory findings into applied analytics by modeling a business scenario for location selection, using bedroom counts, review trends, availability, and pricing to identify suitable properties for long-term, large-group stays.

Nov 2024

Risk-Averse vs. Risk-Seeking Messaging in Airbnb Advertising

Designed and conducted a controlled experimental study using a Qualtrics survey (n = 146) with randomized exposure to risk-averse and risk-seeking advertisements. Analyzed Likert-scale and behavioral intention data using independent-samples t-tests and aggregated risk-tolerance scores, identifying statistically significant gender differences in responses to safety-oriented messaging and overall risk preference. These findings informed data-driven marketing recommendations, emphasizing utilitarian, security-focused messaging for female audiences and hedonic, adventure-oriented messaging for male audiences.
